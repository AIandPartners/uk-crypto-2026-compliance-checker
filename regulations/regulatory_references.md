# UK Crypto Regulatory References 2026

This document provides full citations and access information for regulations referenced in the UK Crypto Regulatory Matrix.

---

## Primary Legislation

### The Financial Services and Markets Act 2000 (Cryptoassets) Regulations 2026 (SI 2026/102)
- **Citation:** FSMA 2026, Crypto Asset Statutory Instrument
- **Status:** Effective 25 October 2027
- **Key Sections:** 
  - Section 47ZA: Statutory trust requirements for client crypto
  - Annex A: Crypto risk management framework
- **Access:** https://www.legislation.gov.uk/uksi/2026/102/pdfs/uksi_20260102_en.pdf
- **Summary:** Establishes core regulatory framework for crypto asset activities in the UK

### Money Laundering, Terrorist Financing and Transfer of Funds (Information on the Payer) Regulations 2017
- **Citation:** MLR 2017
- **Status:** In force from 1 September 2023
- **Key Changes:** Crypto-specific CDD requirements, Travel Rule implementation
- **Access:** https://www.legislation.gov.uk/uksi/2017/692/contents

## FCA Consultation Papers & Policy Statements

### FCA CP25/40: Crypto Asset Regulation
- **Title:** Regulation of Crypto Asset Activities
- **Publication Date:** 16 December 2025
- **Status:** Final rules expected 25 October 2027
- **Key Topics:**
  - Governance requirements (Para 3.8-3.15)
  - Custody and asset protection (Para 5.22-5.45)
  - Marketing and consumer protection (Para 8.12)
  - Staking services (Para 9.8-9.12)
  - Stablecoin requirements (Para 11.15-11.22)
- **Access:** https://www.fca.org.uk/publications/consultation-papers/cp25-40
- **Response Deadline:** 12 February 2026

### FCA PS21/3: Building Operational Resilience
- **Title:** Operational Resilience: Impact tolerances for important business services
- **Extension to Crypto:** Applies to authorised cryptoasset firms through the forthcoming FSMA cryptoasset regime and associated FCA policy statements (expected 2026), rather than via a separate PS21/3 amendment.
- **Access:** [https://www.fca.org.uk/publications/policy-statements/ps21-3](https://www.fca.org.uk/publication/policy/ps21-3-operational-resilience.pdf)

## FCA Handbooks & Rules

### Senior Managers & Certification Regime (SMCR) - Crypto Extension
- **Handbook:** SUP (Supervision) + SYSC (Senior Management Arrangements)
- **Extension Date:** October 2027
- **Crypto-Specific SMFs:** 2026-2027
- **Access:** https://handbook.fca.org.uk/handbook

### Client Assets Sourcebook (CASS) - Crypto Equivalent
- **Working Title:** CASS-Crypto / Digital Asset Client Money Rules
- **Status:** Consultation phase
- **Expected:** 2026-2027
- **Key Requirements:** Segregation, reconciliation, audit trail

### Market Abuse Regulation (MAR) - Crypto Extension
- **Citation:** UK MAR + Crypto Statutory Instrument
- **Effective:** 25 October 2027
- **Covers:** Insider dealing, market manipulation, transaction reporting for crypto
- **Access:** https://www.legislation.gov.uk/eur/2014/596/contents

## Consumer Protection

### FCA Consumer Duty
- **Implementation:** July 2023 (extended to crypto in 2027)
- **Four Outcomes:**
  1. Products and services
  2. Price and value
  3. Consumer understanding
  4. Consumer support
- **Crypto Guidance:** FCA GC23/1 (Consumer Duty Guidance)
- **Access:** https://www.fca.org.uk/firms/consumer-duty

### Financial Promotions Order 2026
- **Citation:** Amendments to the Financial Promotion Order (FPO 2005) and FCA financial promotion rules extending the regime to qualifying cryptoassets.
- **Status:** In force for crypto promotions made by, or approved by, authorised firms; further refinements expected as part of the FSMA cryptoasset regime implementation.
- **Key Changes:** Classifying many crypto promotions as high‑risk investments, Mandatory risk warnings and cooling‑off periods for first‑time retail investors, Stronger frictions (appropriateness tests, “positive frictions” in journeys) for direct‑offer crypto promotions.
- **Access:** https://www.legislation.gov.uk/uksi/2026/102/contents/made

## Anti-Money Laundering

### FATF Recommendation 16 (Travel Rule)
- **Title:** Wire Transfers - Virtual Asset Service Providers
- **UK Implementation:** Via MLR 2026
- **Threshold:** Transfers >£1,000 / $1,000
- **Guidance:** FATF Guidance on Virtual Assets (Updated 2024)
- **Access:** https://www.fatf-gafi.org/

### HM Treasury OFSI - Sanctions Lists
- **Sanctioned Crypto Addresses:** Published lists
- **Access:** https://www.gov.uk/government/organisations/office-of-financial-sanctions-implementation
- **Update Frequency:** Ongoing

## Using These References

When citing in compliance documents:
- Use full citation format
- Include paragraph/section numbers
- Link to official source
- Note publication date (regulations evolve)

**Example Citation:**
> "This policy implements the statutory trust requirement under FSMA 2026, Section 47ZA, as further detailed in FCA CP25/40 Para 5.22-5.28."
